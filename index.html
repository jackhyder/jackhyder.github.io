About Me

I have been an experimenter and tinkerer for my entire life. I tried a career in biochemistry, but after some time I realized my true calling was with software and web engineering.

In late 2016 I decided to take the plunge and put myself on a fast track to learn the skills I would need to transfer into a brand new industry. I attended the Coding Dojo in Bellevue, WA where I launched into a fast-paced and immersive introduction to Web Development. Focusing on 3 languages, I learned the essentials of algorithms, MVC patterns, and full stack applications.

I now spend my time honing my skills, learning new coding techniques, and building real-world projects. If you would like to get in contact with me, shoot me an email or a message through LinkedIn and let's chat!
